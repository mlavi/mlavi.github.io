DevOps Demystified

13/Oct 2015 <<<<<<< HEAD:post/devops_demystified/index.html ======= >>>>>>> cba2fda29bb7381fbb875dfc46eac937fed7c0ef:post/devops_demystified/index.html

For a term that is not even six years old, DevOps remains a mysterious pursuit for many software engineering organizations. Let me define DevOps and help you can understand and chart your own journey to DevOps. Hint: it is a cultural term!

I will polish the slides and elaborate further, but they hammer the key points efficiently! Please contact me with any follow up questions or comments.
